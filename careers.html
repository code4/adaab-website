Head Chef – Hyderabadi Cuisine (Full-Time, Permanent)

Location: 47 Bell Rd, Hounslow, TW3 3NX, United Kingdom

Salary: £38,700 per annum

Adaab Hyderabad is a vibrant restaurant dedicated to bringing the authentic flavours of Hyderabad to the UK. We pride ourselves on traditional recipes, quality ingredients, and a warm dining experience.

Job Description:

We are seeking a passionate and experienced Chef specializing in Hyderabadi and Indian cuisine to lead our kitchen operations and maintain our high culinary standards.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Prepare traditional Hyderabadi Biryani, curries, and tandoor dishes.
  • Supervise kitchen operations and junior staff.
  • Maintain hygiene, food safety, and kitchen organization.
  • Innovate new dishes while preserving authentic flavours.
  • Manage inventory and liaise with suppliers.

Requirements:

  • Minimum 3 years of professional experience in Indian/Hyderabadi cuisine.
  • Expertise in Dum Biryani, Tandoori grilling, and Mughlai dishes.
  • Good leadership and communication skills.
  • NVQ Level 3 in Professional Cookery (or equivalent) preferred.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary.
  • Staff meals provided.
  • Opportunities for career progression.

How to Apply:

Please send your CV and cover letter to hr@adaabhyderabad.co.uk .
